# Impact Of Ai On Students Performance
This is a descriptive and comparative analysis to examine how AI usage among students relates to academic outcomes.

## Brief Overview of the Dataset
This data was sourced from Kaggle.com and it is a public dataset. There are 26 c0lumns which consist of Age, Gender, Grade level and there are 5,000 rows after cleaning.

## Problem Questions 
* Average grades of AI users vs non-users?
* Distribution of student performance level?
* Average improvement rate of Ai users Vs Non Ai Users?
* % of Student that Uses Ai?
* Does the intensity of the use of Ai affect final score?
